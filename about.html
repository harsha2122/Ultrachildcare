About Us

Empowering Children Across Noida & Delhi NCR

At Ultra Childcare, we specialize in early interventions and therapies for children with Autism, Learning Disabilities, ADHD, Down’s Syndrome, and other developmental challenges. Our mission is simple — to help every child build confidence, independence, and life skills through expert care tailored to their unique needs.

  • Services We Offer

    Occupational Therapy, Neurodevelopmental Therapy, Sensory Integration, Speech Therapy, and Pediatric Physiotherapy — all under one roof, designed with children in mind.

  • What We Treat

    Autism Spectrum Disorder, Cerebral Palsy, Developmental Delays, ADHD, Sensory Processing Issues, Learning Disabilities, and more — treated with evidence-based, child-centered approaches.
