Autism Therapy

Comprehensive Autism Therapy

We provide evidence-based autism therapy programs designed to support communication, social interaction, behavioral development, and functional skills for children on the autism spectrum.

Our Therapy Services

  • ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis)
  • Speech and Language Therapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Social Skills Training
  • Sensory Integration
  • Behavioral Intervention
  • Parent Training & Coaching

Focus Areas

Communication: Language development and social communication
Behavior: Managing challenging behaviors and building positive skills
Social: Peer interaction and social understanding
Life Skills: Independence in daily activities

FAQs

Autism is a neurodevelopmental condition affecting communication, social interaction, and behavior. It's a spectrum with varying support needs.

Autism is not a disease but a neurotype. Therapy focuses on developing skills and maximizing potential rather than "curing."

Early intervention is crucial. Starting therapy as soon as diagnosis is confirmed leads to better outcomes.
