Pediatric Occupational Therapy

Pediatric OT Excellence

Specialized occupational therapy for infants, toddlers, and children. We use play-based and developmentally appropriate interventions to support growth, independence, and participation in meaningful activities.

Pediatric OT Focus

  • Developmental milestone achievement
  • Motor skill development (gross and fine)
  • Sensory processing and integration
  • Self-care skill building
  • Play and social skills
  • Academic readiness and performance
  • Behavioral and emotional regulation

Play-Based Approach

Child-Centered: Activities meaningful to the child
Engaging: Therapy through play and fun
Meaningful: Skills that matter in daily life
Family-Focused: Parents as co-therapists

FAQs
