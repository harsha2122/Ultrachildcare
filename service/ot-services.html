OT Services

Comprehensive OT Services

Our complete range of occupational therapy services addresses physical, sensory, cognitive, and psychosocial needs. We provide individualized treatment plans designed to maximize independence and quality of life.

Our OT Service Areas

  • Motor skill development
  • Sensory integration
  • Cognitive rehabilitation
  • Self-care and daily living skills
  • Work and productivity skills
  • Leisure and social skills
  • Adaptive equipment training

Service Delivery Models

Individual Therapy: One-on-one personalized treatment
Group Sessions: Social and peer-based learning
Home Programs: Family-centered intervention
School Consultation: Educational support
